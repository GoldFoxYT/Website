FoodWise-AI

AI-Powered Platform to Reduce Food Waste & Promote Smart Consumption

The Problem We Solve

Food Waste refers to food that is not consumed as intended — whether it's uneaten, leftover, or spoiled.

Causes:

  • Overproduction
  • Inadequate Packaging and Transportation

Impacts:

  • Waste of Natural Resources
  • Harm to Biodiversity

Our Solution

FoodWise-AI is a digital platform powered by Artificial Intelligence that helps restaurants and food services:

  • Track food usage and waste
  • Analyze overproduction or leftover ingredients
  • Recommend inventory and menu improvements
  • Suggest creative uses for surplus (e.g., specials, donations)
  • Connect with organizations that need food

How It Works

  1. Analyze food preparation, usage, and waste data
  2. Highlight meals/items causing the most waste

Impacts

  • Less food waste = more sustainability
  • Lower food costs for restaurants
  • More food for those in need
  • Better decision-making with data

Why FoodWise-AI?

  • Ready to implement — not just an idea
  • Cost-effective and balanced between profit and purpose
  • Highly flexible and scalable
  • Creates both environmental and social impact
